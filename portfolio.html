Interactive Maths Game

Interactive Maths Game...

Maths game intended for primary school kids, challenging their knowledge of Times tables up to 10.

A score was to be given for correct answers with a time limit of 60 secs.

Keeping the end user in mind; simple fonts and bright neutral colours were used.

JavaScript and JQuery were used to generate different questions in succession of correct answers. Timer, wrong and correct answer mechanism was also implemented.

Made use of CSS3 transitions, for button hover, active and click states.
